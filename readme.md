
# Business Profile
A Business Profile defines the CV (resume, curriculum vitae, bio, summary or work history) of your company. It is the marketing document that is prepared to gather interesting parties for your goods and services. Which highlights the basic details, your work experience, reputation, ideas, achievements, date of establishment and contact information.
